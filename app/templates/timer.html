PULSE

Work in short rhythmic BURSTS, focusing on your most important task first. After "starting" your work session, the timer will display 25-minutes; you should FOCUS on the task at hand. Once the timer ends, you will move into a 5-minute break session. Ideally, for the most effective sesssions, complete 4-cycles and then treat yourself to a longer break.

PAUSE

It's critical to include scheduled breaks into your work routine. Building RENEWAL into you work-day, prevents burn-out. It's not only important to REST your mind and eyes; breaking away between work sessions mimics your bodies NATURAL CYCLE for renewal as well. Improve your productivity by allowing yourself to take a break after sessions of intent focus.

The Pomodoro Technique

Move rhythmically between spending and renewing your energy.

work with time

For many people, time is an enemy. We race against the clock to finish assignments and meet deadlines. The Pomodoro Technique teaches you to work with time, instead of struggling against it. A revolutionary time management system, it is at once deceptively simple to learn and life-changing to use.

eliminate burnout

Essential to the Pomodoro Technique is the notion that taking short, scheduled breaks while working eliminates the “running on fumes” feeling you get when you’ve pushed yourself too hard. It’s impossible to over work when you stick to the system. You may end up taking fewer sick days, too!

manage distractions

Whether it’s a call, a Facebook message, or suddenly realizing you need to change the oil in your car, many distracting thoughts and events come up when you’re at work. The Pomodoro Technique will help you avoid distractions and start to associate priority levels to them. Often, they can wait.

work/life balance

Most of us are intimately acquainted with the guilt that comes from procrastinating. If we haven’t had a productive day, it’s pretty easy to end up feeling like we can’t enjoy our free time. Becoming a Pomodoro Master involves creating an effective timetable, allowing you to truly enjoy your time off.
